About Me

I enjoy creating posters that combine strong visuals with clear meaning and intention. For me, design goes beyond aesthetics — it is about communicating ideas, emotions, and stories in a visually compelling way. I continuously experiment with typography, color, and composition to push creative boundaries and develop a distinctive visual language.

Alongside my design journey, I am pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with a specialization in Cybersecurity at NIET, Greater Noida. My academic background has strengthened my analytical thinking and problem-solving abilities, allowing me to approach design with structure, clarity, and attention to detail. By balancing creativity with technical knowledge, I strive to create work that is not only aesthetically strong but also purposeful and strategically crafted.
